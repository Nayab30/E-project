About Us

Our Story

Driven by our deep commitment to the environment and social change, we embarked on a mission as passionate design students. We recognized the growing issue of waste in the event industry and felt compelled to make a difference. Witnessing the immense waste left behind after joyous celebrations saddened us, motivating us to transform the event landscape for the better.


Since our establishment in 2017,Anal has been dedicated to crafting elegant and eco-friendly events that leave a minimal footprint. Our goal is to create awareness among our clients and community about the impact of waste on our planet. By integrating sustainability into every aspect of our events, we strive for

Traditions and Sustainability

Every wedding is a beautiful symphony of customs, rituals, and stories. Our skilled artisans and designers work hand in hand to create exquisite wedding décor and accessories that pay tribute to the timeless traditions of Indian culture while upholding the principles of sustainability.

Our collaborative approach ensures that the artisans' creativity and skill remain at the heart of our designs, fostering economic growth within the community. From handwoven textiles to seasonal flowers, we embrace the magic of local resources to create an enchanting setting for your big day..
